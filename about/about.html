About Claylike Festival

活動簡介

Claylike Festival is a three-day in person event with interactive workshops supporting original Chinese artists to express creativity and uniqueness through their artwork and promoting the significance of Chinese cultural heritage; creating a sustainable Chinese based community and safe space for youths to create freely; celebrating the longevity of Chinese culture.

Our Mission

活動宣言

preserving and reviving valuable traditions that has invaluable history behind. Moreover, encouraging the youths to carry on those cultural heritage and pass to our next generations.
