活動細節

Claylike Festival Will be taken place at Asian Art Museum from May 1st to 3rd to celebrate not only traditional Chinese heritage but also Asian American and Pacific Islander (AAPI) Heritage Month collectiveely. The events are very engaging and interactive manner. Each day of the event will represent a differnt technique that people invented centuries ago, yet still being implemented today whether digitally or physically. There would be workshop set up to help demonstrate and allow participants to have hands on experience.

Day1

Chinese Calligraphy Workshop

Traditional Folk Music

Exhibition of Chinese Calligraphy

Day2

Court Painting Workshop

Clay Art Workshop

Exhibition of Court Painting

Day3

Chinese watercolor workshop

Chinese Symphony

Exhibition of Chinese watercolor drawings
